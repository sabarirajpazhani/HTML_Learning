Gislen Software

Gislen Software is a Software Development Company that provides affordable, quality development and long-term support services to companies and organisations that require leading-edge solutions to mission-critical requirements. We enable our clients to meet strategic business objectives with intelligent technical solutions. We work with them to understand, conceptualise, build and support quality software solutions and products.

Gislen Software was founded in India in 1994 by Mikael Gislen, a Swedish computer professional and businessman who saw the potential in combining European management and commercial expertise with the IT skills, productivity, and value advantages of India. This blend established us as a leading software development company, creating a multi-skilled, multi-cultural team equipped to meet the needs of demanding international clients.
